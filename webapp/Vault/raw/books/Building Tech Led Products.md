# Tech Led Products

* Technology-led products are those whose core value proposition is heavily based on technology
* These products leverage innovative technologies to solve problems, enhance user experiences, and create new market opportunities

[Page 9]
# Key Characteristics

*   **Innovation**: Use of cutting-edge technology to provide unique solutions
*   **User Experience (UX)**: High emphasis on intuitive and seamless user interaction
*   **Scalability**: Ability to handle increasing amounts of work or to be easily expanded
*   **Integration**: Compatibility with other technologies and systems
*   **Personalization**: Ability to customize the offering at scale for every user

[Page 10]
# Tech Led Products

*   Tech Dependency - Tech as core
*   UX Focus: Simplified UX and enhance interaction using tech
*   Scalability: Designed to scale rapidly
*   Personalization: Can Adapt to user behaviour
*   Access to Data: Deep analytics, experimentation, iteration

# Building Tech Led Products

*   User – Who are you solving for?
*   Problem – What is the user's goal and why are they unable to achieve their goal?
*   Solution – How do you intend to solve the problem
*   Plan – What do you build first? How do you build it?
*   Launch and Grow – GTM, Pricing, Monetization
*   Product Analytics – Measure, measure, measure...

YouTube in 2004 – 2005

*   Fastest growing internet site

# PMF needs to be followed by GTM-Fit

*   Narrow your target
*   Focus your product
*   Positioning, Messaging, Communication
*   Packaging and Pricing
*   Channel Alignment

[Page 19]
# Be clear on what to measure

*   Vanity Metrics Vs Utility Metrics
*   Laddering of Metrics is critical
*   Remember – Value creation and value capture can happen at different places - you must measure appropriately

[Page 20]
# To conclude

*   Be clear on the user / problem / opportunity hypothesis
*   Experiment and iterate on the solution – Be data driven / differentiate
*   Product Strategy is critical
*   Think of GTM during product design – You need PMF and GTM-Fit
*   Monetization strategies evolve - experiment and learn
