# Key Takeaways
Platform models are a class of digital business models that shift focus from (supply-side) economies of scale to (demand-side) economies of networks

Key strategic decisions in platform models:
* Permanent subsidization of one side of the platform is often used to mobilize networks
* Assessment of WTA dynamics is important to assess the sustainability of subsidization
* Envelopment is an important strategy of offense by platforms
