## Key Claims

- AI is an overarching field focused on intelligent machines, with Machine Learning as a subset where systems learn from data without explicit programming, and Deep Learning as a further subset using complex neural networks
- AlphaGo's 2016 victory demonstrated that AI could achieve superhuman performance in complex strategic games
- Generative AI (GenAI), emerging prominently in 2022, can generate new data by learning from and mimicking existing data sources
- AI problem types can be categorized into predictive questions (e.g., demand forecasting, customer churn), prescriptive questions (e.g., inventory management, personalized marketing), and generative questions (e.g., AI art, copywriting)
- Many attributes commonly associated with uniquely human intelligence can be demonstrated or mimicked by machines
