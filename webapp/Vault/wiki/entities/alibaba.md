# Alibaba

A company whose Yu'e Bao fund became the world's largest money-market fund, demonstrating how digital initiatives can disrupt traditional industries. A platform business that connects millions of third-party suppliers from China's fragmented markets to a global customer base. A platform company that operates an asset-light model, providing sellers access to a market and supported by its Alipay and Cainiao services.
