# Google

A technology company whose Android platform is an example of an open system and which is a key player in the emerging automotive ecosystem. The technology company that coined the term "Zero Moment of Truth" (ZMOT) to reflect the importance of the online search period before consumers make a purchase. A company that demonstrated the power of big data and uses supervised learning to automatically identify spam emails and translate web pages.
