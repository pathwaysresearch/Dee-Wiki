# Alphabet

A digital giant that has extended its influence into various industries, including automotive through projects like self-driving minivans. A digital giant with a focus on health, life sciences, and longevity that co-created Verb Surgical to redefine surgery. The parent company of Google, under which Nest Labs operates as an independent subsidiary. A digital giant that, through its team DeepMind, is racing to develop and redefine the frontiers of how machines can amplify humans.
