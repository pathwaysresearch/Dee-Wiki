# General Electric (GE)

A company that developed the Predix operating system for the industrial internet and invested over $1 billion to use additive manufacturing for parts in its best-selling jet engines. An industrial incumbent that leveraged its large installed base of machines to create its Predix platform for the internet of things as part of its digital strategy. A company with 300,000 employees that is replacing its traditional annual reviews with an app called PD@GE and is also testing an application to predict employee churn.
