# Deepa Mani

A Professor of Information Systems at the Indian School of Business whose research is at the intersection of technology, organization, and policy. A Professor of Information Systems at the Indian School of Business (ISB) whose research interests include digital transformation and the business value of IT. A Professor at the Indian School of Business who co-hosts the Atrium Talks podcast and researches topics like digital innovation and its relationship with investor expectations. A Professor of Information Systems at the Indian School of Business whose research is at the intersection of technology, organisation, and society, and who has received awards for her work's practical impact. An Assistant Professor of Information Systems at ISB and Executive Director of SRITNE, whose research focuses on the organization and business value of technology investments.
