# Goldman Sachs

An investment bank that shocked the world by launching its SIMON platform and inviting competitors to sell their products on it, exemplifying a platform strategy. A financial firm that opened its SIMON platform to competitors, moving from a single-dealer model to a platform model for its structured-note business. A financial firm that estimates the combined market for AR and VR will be between $80 billion and $182 billion by 2025. A financial firm that underwent a digital transition, starting with improving internal efficiency and evolving to create a platform for structured notes open to competitors. A financial firm that has automated parts of its IPO process, replaced traders with software engineers, and is experimenting with data-driven approaches to talent spotting.
