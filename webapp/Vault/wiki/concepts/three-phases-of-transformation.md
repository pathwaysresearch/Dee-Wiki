# Three Phases of Transformation

A core part of The Digital Matrix framework outlining a step-by-step transformation journey: Experimentation at the Edge, Collision at the Core, and Reinvention at the Root. A model describing the rapid, non-linear evolution of industries in the digital age, consisting of Experimentation at the Edge and Collision at the Core.
