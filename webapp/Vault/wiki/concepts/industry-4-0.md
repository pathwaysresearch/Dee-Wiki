# Industry 4.0

A new era in manufacturing and operations driven by digital technologies such as digital manufacturing, 3-D printing, and digital supply chains to improve operational excellence. A manufacturing paradigm representing a shift from centralized to decentralized production where products communicate with machines to direct their own processing through machine-to-machine communication. The digitization of manufacturing, which includes smart factories and the industrial internet, aimed at achieving operational excellence.
