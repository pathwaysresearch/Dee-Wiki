# Experimentation at the Edge

The first phase of digital transformation, where embryonic digital ideas evolve from concepts to prototypes, pilots, and products. A phase of digital transformation defined as the systematic process of learning by connecting disparate trends and developing experiments to explore how digitization could shape an industry's future. The first of three phases of digital transformation in the Rules Matrix framework. The first of three evolutionary phases in the Rules Matrix, where a company is observing and experimenting with technologies like automation. A phase of the Digital Matrix that serves as a learning opportunity for companies, involving various types of experiments such as challenge-focused and complementary.
