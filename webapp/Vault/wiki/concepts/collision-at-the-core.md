# Collision at the Core

The second phase of digital transformation where proven experiments become business options, creating tension as new digital rules challenge traditional industry practices. A phase of digital transformation where digital products challenge traditional ones and new organizational models based on computer science principles clash with industrial-age companies. A period of intense conflict where the business models of incumbent firms are challenged by newer models from tech entrepreneurs and digital giants. The second of three phases of digital transformation in the Rules Matrix framework. The second phase of digitization where business models collide, and the key challenge for incumbents is to coexist with hybrid traditional and digital models while preparing to morph. The second of three evolutionary phases in the Rules Matrix, where a strategy like amplification becomes important to shift the business model.
