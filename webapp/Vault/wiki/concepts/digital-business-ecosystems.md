# Digital Business Ecosystems

A system where participants like incumbents, tech entrepreneurs, and digital giants are inextricably linked and must work together or compete during industry transformation. Dynamic networks of companies, partners, and customers whose relationships and structures are rapidly changing due to digital transformation.
