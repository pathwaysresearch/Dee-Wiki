# Cognitive Computing

A technology theme, largely synonymous with artificial intelligence, focused on enabling computers to reason through complex paths and frame questions from answers. A system rooted in analytics that process natural language, analyze text, and use knowledge and reasoning to understand complex information, rank hypotheses, and learn from mistakes.
