# Omnichannel Strategy

An approach to serving customers across multiple channels, exemplified by Finansbank creating a new, purely digital brand (Enpara) to serve a tech-savvy segment not catered to by its physical branches. A business strategy involving the integration of different shopping methods for consumers, such as an online retailer like Amazon opening physical stores. An approach that integrates a company's physical and digital channels to create a unified customer experience, treating the channels as complements.
