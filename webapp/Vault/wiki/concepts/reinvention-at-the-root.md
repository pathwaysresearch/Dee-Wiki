# Reinvention at the Root

A phase of digital transformation focused on strengthening business intuition with tangible support from data and analysis to develop insights and apply them with agility. A business transformation stage that involves going beyond overlaying digital technologies to fundamentally reinventing the business model at its core. The third of three phases of digital transformation in the Rules Matrix framework. The third of three evolutionary phases in the Rules Matrix, where a company strives to develop a strong digital business foundation through strategies like augmentation. A strategic concept involving the fundamental rethinking of business models, as exemplified by transformations in industries like automotive and health care.
