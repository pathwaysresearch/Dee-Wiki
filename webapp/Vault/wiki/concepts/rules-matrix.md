# Rules Matrix

A framework for industry incumbents that maps the three phases of digital transformation against three winning moves to create nine actionable rules. A 3x3 grid that forms the basis of the Digital Business Rulebook, organizing nine rules across three evolutionary phases: Experimentation at the Edge, Collision at the Core, and Reinvention at the Root.
