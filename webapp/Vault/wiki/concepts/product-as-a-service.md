# Product-as-a-Service

A business model where a company is paid for the outcome or usage of a product rather than selling the product itself, retaining ownership and maintenance responsibility. A business model where revenue is received over time through recurring payments rather than from a large up-front sale, making customer retention critical. A business model where customers pay for the use or outcome of a product through subscriptions or pay-per-use fees, rather than through ownership.
