# Tech Entrepreneurs

New companies, often with deep and focused expertise in specific vertical areas, that can become formidable future competitors or partners to incumbents. A category of business actor with a specific vertical focus that targets a particular industry problem, sometimes helping incumbents and sometimes challenging them.
