# Digital Matrix

A framework that explains the three types of players in new ecosystems and the different phases of digital transformation. A strategic framework that analyzes business transformation based on the decisions and actions of three sets of players (business-pull) rather than focusing on technology (technology-push). A framework for understanding how digital transformation changes traditional views of competition and cooperation, particularly between industrial companies and digital giants. A core framework that views digital transformation through the actions of three types of players across three distinct phases. A multi-dimensional framework for business transformation that serves as a starting point, focusing on actions and dynamics with distinct phases and players.
