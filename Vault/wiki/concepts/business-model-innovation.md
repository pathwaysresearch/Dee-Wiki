# Business Model Innovation

A fundamental shift in how a business creates and captures value, which can be more impactful than purely technological innovation, as exemplified by Apple's iTunes strategy. The necessary process for businesses to adapt to new technologies by experimenting with and tuning their models, rather than simply applying new tools to old processes.
