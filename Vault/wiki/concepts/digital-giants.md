# Digital Giants

Large technology companies that have extended their influence beyond their traditional industries and may partner with or compete against incumbents. A category of company with large scale and scope ambitions whose experiments typically involve finding new ways to increase scale or expand into new domains. A category of players within the Digital Matrix, characterized by horizontal expertise and the use of powerful machines for experimentation.
