# The Digital Matrix

A conceptual lens for understanding the new business game of the digital future, which involves unknown rules, dynamic coalitions, and exponential trajectories. A framework with two axes, players (Digital Giant, Industry Incumbent, Tech Entrepreneur) and phases (Experimentation, Collision, Reinvention), to help a company understand its position in the digital landscape. A framework describing the complex and dynamic intersection of three types of business players and three phases of digital transformation.
