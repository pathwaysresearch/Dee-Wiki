# Platform Business Model

A business strategy that involves shifting from only selling one's own products to creating a platform where other companies, including competitors, can sell their products. A business model that reduces transaction costs by connecting multiple parties, such as buyers and sellers, and aggregating dispersed supply and demand. A business model that enables transactions between multiple parties, where opening the platform to competitors can increase the overall market size and create a unique competitive advantage.
