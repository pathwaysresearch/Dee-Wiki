# Additive Manufacturing (3-D Printing)

A production technology that allows for the creation of more complex, better, lighter, and more durable products at a lower cost, such as advanced jet engine fuel nozzles. A production method, also known as 3-D printing, that builds objects layer-by-layer and enables on-demand production as part of Industry 4.0.
