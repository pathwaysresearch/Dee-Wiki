# Ecosystem Orchestration

A digital-age business strategy where a company controls a market by assembling and managing a network of product and service providers, defining how value is created and divided. The strategic role of leading and defining the business architecture of an ecosystem, which is not a static role and must be earned. The strategic act of creating and managing new business ecosystems with partners who believe in a shared platform or future.
