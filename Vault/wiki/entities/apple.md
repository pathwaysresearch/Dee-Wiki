# Apple

A technology company whose platform-based model for the iPhone creates high switching costs and whose strategy of hiring auto engineers illustrates the blurring of industry boundaries. A technology company whose tightly controlled iOS is an example of a closed system, and which is a key participant in the mobile payment and automotive ecosystems.
