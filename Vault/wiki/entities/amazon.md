# Amazon

An online retailer that successfully expanded its business scope around customer needs into disparate areas like cloud computing (AWS), hardware (Kindle, Echo), and content creation (Amazon Studios). A company that evolved from an online retailer to a platform by adding a marketplace for third-party sellers, and which effectively uses a complementary products strategy. A company that uses data analytics for its demand-driven supply chain, has patented a 'flying warehouse,' and uses robots in its fulfillment centers. A company that has expanded from online retail into devices and physical stores as part of an omnichannel strategy. An e-commerce company that exemplifies dynamic pricing by testing several different prices for many products on the same day. A company with a broad business scope that operates as a platform, utilizes an omnichannel strategy, and benefits from network effects and core competencies in logistics.
