# Jeff Immelt

The CEO of General Electric who led the company's digital transformation to improve industrial productivity through software and analytics. The CEO of GE in 2016 who announced plans to build a $1 billion business in additive manufacturing equipment and service by 2020. The leader of GE who realized that the company's large installed base of machines provided a significant advantage for creating an internet of things platform.
